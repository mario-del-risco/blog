> *Sing it to me in a song, oh street lady! For I am too broken and done, oh street lady! That’s why I keep making this songs, One by one, sweet lady.*

  

It’s time to look in the invisible mirror, in the color-less pond. Before you knew of it, it was there. After you sing about it, it says hello. No miracles can that pond away, its just reflection. However, there you will find the Great Mother. And the shine and splendor will baffle your heart
